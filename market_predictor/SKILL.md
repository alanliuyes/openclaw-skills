# Market Predictor Skill

通用多资产市场预测分析工具。当用户请求预测任意资产（股票、ETF、LOF、QDII、大宗商品、指数、基金等）的涨跌概率时触发。

## Usage

Trigger keywords: 预测、涨跌概率、市场分析、即时、近期、中期、长期、上涨下跌、概率表格、投资机会

Use this skill for:
- Predicting price movements
- Analyzing market trends
- Probability calculations
- Investment opportunity analysis

## Supported Assets

- Stocks
- ETFs
- LOFs
- QDII
- Commodities
- Indices
- Funds
